The Perfect Steak Recipe We Provide to You

Steak Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 thick-cut steak
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over high heat.
  3. Add a drizzle of olive oil to the skillet.
  4. Cook the steak for 3-5 minutes per side for medium-rare.

Enjoy your delicious steak!
